Privacy Notice

Last updated: [Insert date]

The National Petroleum Council (“NPC”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to handling information about visitors to this website in a responsible manner. This Privacy Notice explains, in brief, what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information in two ways:

  • Information you provide – for example, when you contact us by email or through a form, you may provide your name, organization, email address, or other details.
  • Information collected automatically – when you visit our site, we collect technical information such as your IP address, browser and device type, pages visited, links clicked, and the date and time of your visit.

2. Use of Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use this site and to improve our content and usability. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as:

  • Which pages are visited and for how long
  • Links and downloads that are clicked
  • Approximate geographic region, browser, and device type

This information is processed in aggregate and is not used by us to identify individual users. Google may process this data on servers located in the United States and may use it in accordance with its own privacy policies.

You can control or disable Google Analytics cookies through your browser settings. You may also use browser add-ons or tools that block analytics tracking if you prefer.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Monitor site performance and security
  • Understand how visitors access and use our reports and materials
  • Respond to inquiries you choose to send to us
  • Prepare non-identifiable usage statistics for internal reporting

We do not use this website to serve advertising, create marketing profiles, or sell personal information.

4. Sharing and Retention

We may share aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics with government partners, contractors, or working groups who assist us in operating this site or evaluating its reach. We do not sell or rent personal information.

Web logs and analytics data are retained only as long as necessary for security, operational, and reporting purposes, and are then deleted or anonymized where feasible.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information collected through this website. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. External Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites, including government agencies, research organizations, and other external resources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy statements separately.

7. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or how information is handled in connection with this website, you may contact:
