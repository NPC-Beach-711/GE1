Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2025

The National Petroleum Council (“NPC”, “we”, or “us”) collects limited information from visitors to this site. This Notice explains what we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: such as your name, email address, and organization when contacting us.
  • Automatically collected information: IP-derived geolocation, browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand site usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to measure:

  • pages visited and time spent
  • files downloaded
  • browser, device type, and general region

This data is aggregated and not used to identify individuals.

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve the website’s usability and performance
  • Respond to inquiries you submit
  • Prepare non-identifying usage statistics

4. Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. Aggregated analytics may be shared with government partners or contractors.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect collected data, though no system is completely secure.

6. External Links

External websites linked from this site are not governed by this notice.

7. Changes

Updates to this Privacy Notice will be posted with a revised date.
